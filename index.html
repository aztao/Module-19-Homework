As the population of Austin grows, open space and access to it via additional trails are vital to the health of our community. Join Hill Country Conservancy to help us preserve lands that ensure water quantity and quality for our citizens and to help us build the 30-mile Violet Crown Trail!

Want To Make A Donation?

There are several ways to give to Hill Country Conservancy. Find the way that makes the most sense for you.

Ways to Give

Make A One-Time Donation

Whether you can give $5 or $5,000, making a one-time donation is a great way to show your support for HCC right now.

Become A Monthly Supporter

Wellspring: a source of continual support. An investment in Wellspring is an investment in quality of life. Our focus is on conservation, access to nature, and clean water for all Central Texans through a monthly gift. Your support on a monthly basis will help HCC sustain our work! Once you make your initial donation, future donations will be charged automatically from your account on the same day each month.

Planned Giving

The term “planned giving” covers a wide range of giving opportunities that range from bequests to gifts of stock or personal property. Most planned gifts are deferred gifts, designated to your chosen beneficiary after death. A planned gift is the perfect way to leave a legacy that will be here forever. To learn more about making a planned gift, please contact Fleetwood Jacobs at (512) 328-2481 x202 or fleetwood@hillcountryconservancy.org
